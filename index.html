Visit Owerri

Enjoy vibrant nightlife and rich Igbo culture in the heartland of eastern Nigeria.

Top three activities to do in Owerri

Dive into Igbo culture

The Mbari Cultural and Art Centre is an open-air museum that showcases artifacts that celebrate Igbo culture and history.

Experience the renowned nightlife

Experience Owerri's vibrant nightlife in lounges like Ibari Ogwa and Channel O.

Savor the local cuisine

Ofe Owerri is a rich, traditional Igbo soup, named after the city itself. It is known for its expensive and assorted ingredients.
